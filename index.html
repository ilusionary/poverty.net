Poverty Around the World

About Poverty

Poverty is a global issue affecting millions of people around the world. It is defined as the state of having little or no money, goods, or means of support.

Causes of Poverty

  • Unemployment
  • Lack of education
  • Natural disasters
  • Economic instability

Solutions to Poverty

There are several ways to combat poverty, including:

  1. Providing access to education
  2. Creating job opportunities
  3. Improving healthcare
  4. Ensuring fair wages
